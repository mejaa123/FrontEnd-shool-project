What license do I need to drive your campervans?

You only need a valid category B (standard car) driver’s license, valid in the EU.

What is included in the rental price?

Our packages include the campervan, insurance, roadside assistance, kitchen equipment, and bedding. See package details for more.

Can I travel outside Finland with the campervan?

Yes! With the Advanced Explorer Package, travel is allowed across Nordic countries. Just let us know in advance.

Is there a mileage limit?

Nope! All our rentals come with unlimited mileage—adventure without boundaries.

Are pets allowed in the campervans?

Yes, pets are welcome in select vehicles. Please inform us during booking so we can accommodate you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 7 days before your trip. Cancellations closer to departure may incur a fee—check our full policy for details.
